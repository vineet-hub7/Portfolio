02 · Digital / FPGA

UART Transmitter

Designed and implemented a UART transmitter from scratch in Verilog with an internal baud rate generator derived from the system clock. Designed a 4-state FSM (IDLE, START, DATA, STOP) ensuring protocol-correct 8N1 serial framing with LSB-first data serialisation via a shift register datapath. Verified end-to-end using a self-checking testbench in Xilinx Vivado with waveform-level timing analysis.

03 · RF / Analog

2G GSM Mobile Signal Jammer

Designed a 900 MHz RF interference circuit using an NE555 astable multivibrator as the modulation source driving a BF495 RF transistor in common-emitter topology. Designed an LC tank oscillator with air-core inductors and trimmer capacitors achieving tunable output across the 880–960 MHz GSM band. Analysed transistor biasing (Q-point, VCE, IC) and verified via LTspice simulation.
